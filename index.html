I am an inquisitive and creative person who likes coding in Python and Javasript, making Robots, playing Piano and Tennis and learning German. The reason why I like to code is because it allows me to be as creative as I can be. I make coding video tutorials for beginners because knowing that someone learned from my work humbles me. I like making simulators because you need to think about different challenges and the result is fun to make and play around with. Robotics is also one of the things I like because it goes hand-in-hand with coding. I find AI and machine learning very interesting as they open up a new horizon for building useful applications.For relaxation, I play Piano and compose melodies which you can find on my website.Hope you find my work interesting.
